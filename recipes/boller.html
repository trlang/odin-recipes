Boller fra bakeriet i Lom

Norwegian sweet buns

Traditional Norwegian sweet buns from the famous bakery in Lom. Characterized by the use of cardamom and rasins.

This recipe makes 20 rather large buns.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Whole milk
  • Egg
  • Fresh yeast
  • Sugar
  • All purpose flour
  • Cardamom
  • Salt
  • Rasins

Steps

  1. Have all the ingredients except butter in a mixing bowl. Knead for 15 minutes. Add the butter. Knead for another 15 minutes.
  2. Let the dough rest for 40 minutes on the counter
  3. Gently knead your dough. Divide the dough into equal pieces. Roll them into smooth, round, buns. And put on a baking sheet
  4. Let the buns rise to twice their original size.
  5. Brush the buns with a beaten egg, and bake them in the oven at 225 degrees celsius for 10-12 minutes.
